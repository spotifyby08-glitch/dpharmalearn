D-Pharma Examination Pattern

Theory Examination

Duration3 Hours
Total Marks80 Marks
Internal Assessment20 Marks
Passing Marks36 out of 80 (45%)
Question TypesLong answer, Short answer, MCQs

Practical Examination

Duration4 Hours
Total Marks50 Marks
Passing Marks23 out of 50 (45%)
Viva Voce10 Marks included
JournalMandatory submission required

Important Note

Students must pass Theory and Practical separately. Failing Practical means failing the subject even with good Theory marks. Compartment exams are available for students failing in 1‚Äì2 subjects.
